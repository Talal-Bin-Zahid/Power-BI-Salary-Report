## 4. Key Insights from Charts

### Salary by Experience Level & Employment Type
- **Executive (EX)** roles consistently command the **highest salaries**, particularly under **Contract (CT)** and **Full-Time (FT)** arrangements.
- **Entry-level (EN)** and **Mid-level (MI)** salaries are the most common and cluster between **$30K â€“ $100K USD**, depending on role and employment type.
- **Senior (SE)** roles show a significant salary uplift compared to EN and MI, especially in full-time positions.

### Salary by Location
- Salaries are **predominantly clustered in North America and Europe**, with the **United States** showing the highest concentration of both employees and higher average salaries.
- Additional salary activity is visible across parts of **Asia** and **Australia**, though at lower density compared to North America.
